About Our Brand

Ivory Rose is not the booming brand it has always been. I started this business with nothing but my grandma's sewing machine, and a pile of what was supposed to be donation clothes. Sure fast fashion is cute, but is it also recycled?

Textile waste is one of the largest contributers to pollution around the globe. By using second hand clothing and sewing scraps, we are repurposing and giving these textiles a second, more fab life!

Over the years I have met some increadible and passionate designers that share the same goal to reduce, reuse, and recycle textiles!! Bouncing ideas off of eachother and working as a team to work more sustainably has lead us to create a Boutique catered to such!

Each piece is uniquely made by a member of our team to further show the endless possibilities of recycled clothing.
